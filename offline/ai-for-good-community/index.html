Built by the AI for Good Community

Eindhoven Chapter

FruitPunch AI started as a foundation in 2020 to teach engineers at TU Eindhoven how to apply AI for Good. We keep a strong footprint and a large community of volunteers organized in the Eindhoven Chapter. Since its founding, the chapter has educated 1130+ AI enthusiasts and 285+ course followers. The chapter organized 5 AI for Health challenges and more are in the pipeline.
Want to join the Chapter?

South Africa Chapter

Our first and longest running AI for Good Challenge - AI for Wildlife takes place in South Africa. Over the past year we've built up a strong alliance with the top 5 universities of the country to teach AI for Good and solve a multitude of their challenges from water management to power grid optimization to nature conservation.
Want to join the Chapter?

India Chapter

The India Chapter is focusing on tackling health issues such as mental health, fitness and access to healthcare. With its 1.36 billion people and the low level of healthcare spending at 1.23% of GDP, there is a significant impact opportunity in applying AI to improve the health and well-being of Indian population.
Want to join the Chapter?

AI for Wildlife Lab

Labs by FruitPunch AI build a community of AI engineers with an ecosystem of partners around a specific topic and technology. AI for Wildlife Lab bundles the experience from all AI for Wildlife Challenges and resources of partners to enable more machine learning projects for biodiversity protection. The tech in focus is edge computing, tinyML and computer vision.
Learn more

Uganda Chapter

The Uganda Chapter based in Kampala is led by Barbra Apilli (Team Lead) - a computer scientist. Barbara is a software engineer developing healthcare solutions and protecting wildlife in her free time through the AI for Wildlife Challenges. She teamed up with Hewitt Tusiime (Tech Lead) - a research assistant at the Artificial Intelligence and Data Science Lab at Makerere University.
Want to join the Chapter?

Start your own chapter

Can’t find a chapter near you? Why not start one! The first chapter was started because our founder Buster didn't find any opportunity to learn applied AI through real-world projects at his university. Be the change you want to see in the world and start a chapter.

USA Chapter

FruitPunch AI’s first USA chapter based in Chicago aims at cultivating a community of AI Engineers motivated to solve some of the most pressing problems of the world. To achieve this, we plan to form a strong alliance with some of the most reputed universities to educate AI enthusiasts. With a > $100B market value of AI, the US presents an excellent opportunity to bring substantial applications of AI in sectors with the most need around the globe.
